ImagePlot plotting Jordan Belson's Chakra (1972). Graphed by median brightness horizontally (x-axis) and median saturation vertically (y-axis).

This project aims to continue ongoing conversations about recontextualizing and expanding our understanding of how we engage with film, using experimental and avant-garde films as revisualization case studies.

What can we understand from moving image works when we view the images that come together to form the moving image concurrently? What new meanings can we discern from a new form of engagement with moving images?
